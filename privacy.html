Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 April 2026

This policy explains what personal information No More Rubbish Websites ("we", "us") collects when you use nomorerubbishwebsites.com, why we collect it, and what your rights are. We are a UK sole trader and the data controller for any personal data you share with us.

Who we are

No More Rubbish Websites is operated by an individual sole trader based in the United Kingdom. You can contact us at any time at info@nomorerubbishwebsites.com.

What we collect

When you submit the quote request form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Business name (optional)
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • The package you are interested in
  • Anything you tell us in the message field

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising. We do not run analytics scripts that profile you. Your browser will request standard fonts from Google Fonts when you load the page — Google may log your IP address as part of that request.

Why we collect it

We use the information you submit solely to reply to your enquiry, prepare a quote, and (if you become a customer) deliver the work you've asked for. The legal basis is legitimate interest for replying to enquiries and contract once you become a client.

Who we share it with

Form submissions are processed by Web3Forms, which forwards them to our inbox. Email is hosted by our standard mail provider. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone else.

How long we keep it

Enquiries that don't lead to a project are kept for up to 12 months and then deleted. Client records are kept for 6 years after the end of our work together, as required by HMRC for tax purposes.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete, or request a copy of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, email info@nomorerubbishwebsites.com. You can also complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk) if you believe we've mishandled your data.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy we'll update the "last updated" date at the top of the page. Material changes will be flagged on the homepage.
