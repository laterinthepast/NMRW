Our Story

A Proper Glasgow Local

The Anchor & Cleaver has been feeding and watering Glasgow since 2009. We took a forgotten Victorian boozer on Miller Street and brought it back to what a pub should be — great food, honest drink, and somewhere you actually want to stay.

Our head chef sources locally wherever possible — Perthshire beef, Hebridean seafood, Ayrshire dairy. The menu changes with the seasons, but the welcome never does.

Whether it's a quiet weeknight dinner, Saturday with the family, or the World Cup final at full volume — there's always a seat for you at the Anchor.
