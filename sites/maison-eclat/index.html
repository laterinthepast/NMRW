Our Story

Twelve Years on
Byres Road.

Maison Éclat opened its doors in 2013 with one simple belief: every client deserves to feel seen. Not rushed in and out, not given a standard cut — but genuinely listened to and looked after.

Twelve years on, we're still the same small, passionate team. We've done hundreds of weddings, thousands of colours, and built lasting relationships with clients who've been with us from the beginning.

Our Byres Road salon is our home. Come in for a coffee, a chat, and the best colour in the West End.
